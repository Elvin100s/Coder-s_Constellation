"Let me start by explaining the problem we're solving.

**The Problem:** Across Africa, passionate developers struggle to find meaningful collaboration. Without a structured platform, they resort to generic 'Looking for collaborators' posts on WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or Twitter - with little to no response.

**Why is this a problem?**

1. **Professional Isolation** - Developers can't find like-minded teammates
2. **Missed Opportunities** - Talented developers remain invisible outside their immediate networks
3. **Fragmented Tools** - Communication is scattered across multiple disconnected platforms
4. **Project Failure** - Even when teams form at hackathons, they often disband due to lack of ongoing communication channels

According to the World Bank, only 1.5% of African youth aged 15-24 are enrolled in formal vocational training. This limits the continent's potential for technology-driven growth and economic inclusion."
